WHAT'S IN THE BOX?

This weeks content was an introduction to Arduino! Ardunio is an open-source electronics platform based on easy-to-use hardware and software. Arduino boards are able to read inputs (eg. light on a sensor, a finger on a button, or a Twitter message) and turn it into an output. This is my first time working with physical computing and electronics.

BLINK!

Once having bearings on the Arduino, we completed an Arduino circut in class. We coded a buzzer to blink when the ultrasonic sensor component detects an object in front of it. The process consists of recreating the circut diagram and coding in Arduino IDE on my laptop.
